performance.smooth_note¶

performance.smooth_note(x, x_mid, y_mid)[source]¶

Generate a smoothed trajectory of a note by connecting the midpoints between peaks and troughs.

Parameters:

  • x – Inputted signal.

  • x_mid – Midpoint locations in x axis between peaks and troughs.

  • y_mid – Midpoint locations in y axis between peaks and troughs.

Returns:

Smoothed version of inputted signal x.
